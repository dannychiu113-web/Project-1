About This Page

Facts about steak

  • Steak is a cut of meat, typically beef, that is sliced perpendicular to the muscle fibers.
  • It can be cooked using various methods, including grilling, pan-frying, and broiling.
  • Steak is often seasoned with salt and pepper before cooking.
  • The doneness of steak can range from rare to well-done, depending on personal preference.
  • Popular cuts of steak include ribeye, sirloin, filet mignon, and T-bone.

How to Cook Steak

  1. Choose your preferred cut of steak.
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat your grill or pan to high heat.
  4. Cook the steak for a few minutes on each side, depending on the desired doneness.
  5. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
